About Us

Fur Babes was developed by a college student who wants to help others secure their financial future by making a mark on it today. Our developer is also a pet and anime enthusiast which prompted the visual art design behind the project. She wanted to create a trustworthy community in NFT space for like-minded individuals. Fur Babes will be a place where we can grow together, share ideas and make an impact in the real world. Fur Babes has the consensus to make contributions to charities for the benefit of our furry friends and those who seek higher education. We will also be actively building, growing our community and implementing project goals.

What is an NFT?

NFTs or Non-Fungible Tokens, are digital assets on blockchains with unique identification codes and metadata that distinguish them from each other. NFTs are unique and not mutually interchangeable, which means no two NFTs are the same. These crypto-assets can be unique artwork, music, collectibles, in-game items or more.

What makes an NFT Valuable?

When an item is scarce, unique, or in demand, generally it becomes more valuable. The value of an NFT comes from the property it represents, which exists in the digital world like an original piece of art or digital memorabilia. There are many copies of the Mona Lisa, but only one original.

How do I buy and sell an NFT?

Open marketplaces, like OpenSea.io, are popular for buying and trading NFTs. Open marketplaces allow anyone to buy, sell and trade on their platform.

Where does my NFT go after Purchase?

After your purchase is complete, your NFT will be transferred to your wallet. You will be able to see the contents of your wallet on your Opensea profile, and some wallets display images of your NFTs as well.

When is the mint date? How many can I mint?

Fur Babes Launch is TBD. Mint will be announced on socials. There will be 15,000 Fur Babes in the collection to take home. Each wallet will be able to mint 2 babes.
